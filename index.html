Purpose Tutoring

JD is the founder of Purpose Tutoring, a thriving tutoring company he grew from a solo practice into a team of tutors who support students nationally across a wide range of subjects.

Purpose Tutoring's mission is to help students become irreplaceable in a world that is increasingly trying to replace human intelligence. JD believes that when students learn to think clearly, solve problems independently, and trust their own minds, better grades, higher test scores, and stronger college opportunities follow as a natural result.

As part of his own tutoring practice, JD works with students in advanced calculus, SAT and ACT prep, and college admissions. His team supports students in K-12 math, science, English, foreign languages, study skills, SAT/ACT prep, college admissions, and more.

Since expanding Purpose Tutoring into a multi-tutor practice, JD has dedicated more of his energy to growing the company into a successful enterprise that allows him to support himself, pay his tutors well above market rate, and deliver an exceptional experience to every client.

One of JD's core business philosophies is that paying people well is both the right thing to do and a smart business strategy. When tutors are well compensated, they are happier, more motivated, and more invested in doing excellent work. That leads to better tutoring, happier clients, more repeat bookings, and a stronger business for everyone involved.

Queen City Toastmasters

JD serves as president of Queen City Toastmasters, a public speaking organization that, under his leadership, has grown into the largest public speaking club in the Carolinas, reaching more than 70 active members.

During his presidency, the club has experienced unprecedented competitive success, finishing the contest season with a perfect 8-0 record. Queen City Toastmasters won first place in both the District Impromptu Speaking Championship and the Prepared Speaking Championship, outlasting 87 other clubs. The club is currently awaiting confirmation to proceed to the World Championship Semifinals in Vancouver.

Through his own speeches, JD inspires audiences to think deeply, lead courageously, and find their own path. He is known for drawing from his personal story, connecting naturally with listeners, and creating an energetic sense of engagement in the room.

JD's Speaking Engagements

JD brings extraordinary energy, warmth, and passion to every stage. His speaking style combines charisma with a clear, articulate, and commanding presence, allowing him to connect deeply with audiences and leave them feeling inspired, motivated, and ready to act.

JD is available for keynote talks, leadership workshops, school assemblies, guest lectures, company programs, entrepreneurship panels, team-building events, and private organizational functions.

JD specializes in motivational topics centered on leadership, teamwork, authenticity, and courage. Drawing from his personal story and entrepreneurial experience, he is especially effective at speaking to business audiences, school groups, and organizations looking to inspire growth, confidence, and meaningful action.
